Key combos are like(ctrl, shift, etc.) plus we have to add one regular key (letter keys, number keys, arrow keys, etc), all joined with + signs.

Examples:

    e
    ctrl+h (hold ctrl and then press h)
    shift+2(hold shift and then press 2)

In addition to regular letters, numbers, and punctuation, the following special keys can be used for shortcuts: backspace, tab, clear, enter, return, space, up, down, left, right, end,del, delete.
